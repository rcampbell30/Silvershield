Silver Shield settings

Set up local family support and warning preferences.

Privacy first

Silver Shield checks messages locally in your browser in this MVP. Your checked messages are not sent to a server. Avoid pasting passwords, bank details, or verification codes.

Trusted contact

This is stored only on this browser. Silver Shield does not send messages to this person yet.

Emergency guidance

  • If money was sent, contact your bank immediately using the number on the back of your card.
  • If login details were shared, change the password from the official website or app.
  • If a verification code was shared, contact the service directly and tell them what happened.
  • In the UK, scams and fraud can be reported to Action Fraud.
