Silver Shield Privacy Policy

Summary: Silver Shield v0.1.0 checks scam warning signs locally in your browser. It does not send checked messages, page text, or browsing data to a server.

What Silver Shield does

Silver Shield is a browser extension that helps users identify scam-like warning signs in suspicious messages, links, and web pages.

Local-only checking

In v0.1.0, scam-risk checking runs locally in the browser. There is no Silver Shield backend, account system, telemetry, payment system, or external AI/API call.

What may be processed locally

  • Text pasted into the popup checker.
  • Links pasted into the popup checker.
  • Visible page text when page warning banners are enabled.
  • The current page URL/hostname for local trusted and blocked domain checks.

What is stored locally

  • Trusted contact details.
  • Protected person setup details.
  • Warning sensitivity and page-warning preferences.
  • Trusted domains and blocked domains.
  • Sites where warning banners were dismissed.
  • Recent-check history with short previews only.

What is not collected

  • No full checked messages are sent to a server.
  • No page text is sent to a server.
  • No browsing history is collected.
  • No analytics or telemetry is collected.
  • No account data is collected.
  • No payment data is collected.

Sensitive information

Users should avoid pasting passwords, bank details, PINs, one-time passcodes, or verification codes into Silver Shield or any other tool.

Future AI checking

Optional AI checking is not part of v0.1.0. Any future AI feature would require explicit consent and clear explanation of what data would be sent.

Contact and support

For support, use the project support page or GitHub repository associated with Silver Shield.
