Family scam safety checklist

Pause, check, and ask before sending money or sharing details.

  • Never share verification codes, PINs, passwords, or one-time passcodes.
  • Never move money because of an urgent message, phone call, or pop-up.
  • Never pay parcel fees from a text-message link.
  • Call the bank using the number on the back of the card.
  • Contact HMRC, DVLA, NHS, or delivery companies through official websites only.
  • Do not install AnyDesk, TeamViewer, or remote access tools because a stranger told you to.
  • If a family member messages from a new number, call their old number or ask another family member.
  • If an offer promises guaranteed returns, pause and ask someone you trust.
  • Ask your trusted contact before sending money, buying gift cards, or paying fees.

If something already happened

Contact your bank immediately using the official number on your card or banking app. Keep screenshots and do not use phone numbers from the suspicious message.
